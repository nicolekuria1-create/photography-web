My Favorite Photos

These images are a reminder to live in the moment, to notice the beauty most people pass by, and to give presence to those who feel unseen,because every soul deserves to be noticed.

  1. Escape From Reality View full photo

    Some hearts grow tired of carrying the weight of the world, and begin to chase silence in anything that can numb the noise. For someone living with addiction, escape can feel like borrowed freedom but real peace begins where healing, truth, and support are allowed in.

  2. Between Noise and Silence View full photo

    Some people fight quiet battles with their mental health, yet still show up with a smile and perform strength for everyone around them. This image is for the ones healing in private while carrying others in public your pain is real, your effort is seen,you are loved just as much as you love others and your softness is not weakness .

  3. You Are Enough View full photo

    This comes from one of my favourite movie called 'Waves'(2019), which depicts how people go and chase the rush of love without even finding it in themselves first. The two characters, in my opinion, are both worn out with the chase, but because it is the only thing that makes them feel alive, they keep going for more, which leaves both at the end miserable, dead and alive.

  4. At Last View full photo

    I think the world of today has reduced the purpose of life to capitalism and that leaves people forgetting what life is even about anymore. You yes you were never meant to stick to one path of life that you chose during your teenage years till you grow old. There is a whole world you are yet to see, people you are yet to meet and many more. It's all about taking one step at a time and enjoying what life has to offer. SONDER is what describes this all perfectly.

Short Biography of the Website

Beyond the Lens into the Heart was created to celebrate moments that pass quickly but stay with us forever and is from the heart of an empath who wants to potray as much as they feel.

My name is Nicole Kuria, and I focus on emotions, feelings and conveying the heavy that may sometimes lack words to be expressed. I build this page to share my best work, improve my communication skills, and connect with people who love visual art.

  • Mission: Preserve the beauty of everyday moments.
  • Style: Emotional and cinematic imagery.
  • Goal: Keep improving both photography and design.
